Healthy Chunky Monkey Ice Cream

Home

Description

A healthy banana based ice cream. Total prep time is about 8 hours and 5 minutes.

A healthy and delicious recipe that is very easy to make.

Ingredients

    2 bananas, chopped
    1 tablespoon 1% milk
    1 tablespoon chunky peanut butter
    1/2 ounce dark chocolate, chopped

Steps

Step 1

    -Place chopped bananas in freezer until frozen, 8 hours to overnight.

Step 2

    -Blend frozen bananas, milk, peanut butter, and chocolate in a blender until smooth.
